About HREA

History

The HREA Work Festival is designed to develop an agile, innovative and future-ready human workforce. The Festival comprises multiple events with one overarching mission to deepen the reflection on the future world of work, and how to face the challenges and grasp the opportunities it may bring. Featuring world renowned speakers drawn from various industries of specialization, participants will experience breath-taking plenary sessions, themed stages, innovation village and a world-class exhibition center.

The Transition of a Ground Breaking Conference

Since 2016, HR Expo Africa has been organizing one of the biggest human resource conferences in Africa. Having attracted over 3000 participants, featured over 200 stellar speakers and over 1000 organizations from across the continent, the conference has sparked transformational conversations and catalysed the reawakening of HR to play more pivotal roles in the organizations.

After a higly-acclaimed fourth edition of the conference which featured many great speakers, HREA is now transitioning to help individuals and organizations scale through the new realities of the Fourth Industrial Revolution.
